## 删除的时候不能使用子查询  


错误: 

~~DELETE FROM tableA WHERE ID in (SELECT ID FROM tableB WHERE XXX)~~

正确写法

DELETE a FROM tableA a LEFT JOIN tableB b on a.ID = b.ID WHERE B.XXX  

PS: 重点在于delete 后面的那个表 指定删除哪个表
